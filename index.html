About

Your workshop photo

I'm a woodworker based in Wisconsin, specializing in custom furniture and built-ins. What started as a hobby in my garage has grown into a passion for creating functional, beautiful pieces that last generations.

Every project begins with understanding what you need and how you live. Whether it's a dining table for family gatherings or custom storage that fits your exact space, I work with you to design and build something that's uniquely yours.

I document my process and share techniques on YouTube to help other makers learn and improve their craft. There's something satisfying about working with wood – each piece has its own character and challenges.
