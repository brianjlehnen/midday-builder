About

Midday Builder is a one-man furniture shop based in Wisconsin.

I handcraft modern furniture with clean lines and minimalist details. Everything's built in my shop from start to finish—no shortcuts, no mass production.

I'm still learning and growing as a maker, documenting the journey as I go. You'll see the real process: the wins, the fails, and everything in between. Building furniture is problem-solving, and I think there's value in showing the process.

My goal is simple: create furniture that's beautiful with intention.
